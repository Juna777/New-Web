About

Tree Shade Resort and Spa

Situated in the charming coastal town of Moalboal, Treeshade Resort & Spa offers a tranquil and idyllic retreat for vacationers. The resort enjoys a prime location, just a short walk away from two beautiful beaches - Basdiot Beach and Panagsama Beach. This provides guests with easy access to the sun, sand, and crystal-clear waters that Moalboal is renowned for.

Upon entering the resort, guests are greeted by a lush and well-maintained garden that exudes a sense of serenity and tranquility. As they settle into their rooms, they will find themselves surrounded by modern comforts and conveniences. Certain units in the resort boast a private terrace, offering stunning vistas of the inviting pool and creating a perfect setting for relaxation.The air-conditioned rooms are tastefully designed, incorporating elements of comfort and style. Guests will find amenities such as a spacious wardrobe, a kettle for their convenience, a safety deposit box for securing valuables, and a flat-screen TV for entertainment purposes. Each room also features a private balcony, allowing guests to enjoy breathtaking views of their surroundings. The en-suite bathrooms are equipped with a bidet, ensuring a refreshing and comfortable stay.

Treeshade Resort & Spa offers its guests a delightful culinary experience with its Ã  la carte breakfast options. Guests can indulge in a variety of mouthwatering dishes that are freshly prepared and served with utmost care and attention. Exploring the nearby attractions is a breeze from the resort's convenient location. Nature enthusiasts will be delighted to know that the renowned Kawasan Falls is just 27 km away, offering an unforgettable waterfall experience amidst lush greenery. Additionally, history buffs can visit the Santo Nino Church, a significant religious landmark situated 21 km from the resort.

For those arriving by air, Sibulan Airport serves as the nearest gateway to the resort, located approximately 80 km away. The resort offers free private parking for guests who choose to drive, ensuring a hassle-free arrival and departure experience. Treeshade Resort & Spa Moalboal is a hidden gem that offers a perfect blend of relaxation, natural beauty, and modern comforts. Whether it's unwinding by the pool, exploring the picturesque beaches, or embarking on exciting adventures nearby, guests are guaranteed an unforgettable stay in this tropical paradise.
