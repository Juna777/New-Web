SCUBA DIVING
Moalboal is at the heart of marine bio-diversity in the Philippines and its healthy reefs and steep walls support schools of snappers, jacks, barracudas, sardines, reef sharks, tuna, turtles, passing thresher sharks and even occasional whale sharks.

SARDINE RUN
The Moalboal sardine run is something that you definitely don’t want to miss when traveling on Cebu.
In fact, the sardine run in Moalboal is one of the reasons why tourism kicked off so rapidly in this awesome little beachside town.

SNORKELING
If you’re reading this to try to find where the best place to see turtles in Moalboal , then you haven’t been in the water yet.
Moalboal is famous for its large turtles that frequent the waters. In fact, there are many that you will struggle to NOT find a turtle while snorkeling in Moalboal.

CANYONEERING
Canyoneering (or canyoning) is a popular outdoor trek that involves traveling down creeks or streams within a canyon through variety of means including walking, jumping, swimming and climbing.
It is one of the most rewarding and fun excursions offered in Moalboal, Cebu, Philippines, rappelling down pristine waterfalls into crystal clear fresh water rivers. A once in a lifetime opportunity and one you will never forget.
